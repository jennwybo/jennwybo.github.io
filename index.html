In New York State 2021, Covid cases were highest in January

New York State Covid data studies show that Covid-19 Cases were the highest in the winter months, when residents tended to stay indoors. January of 2021 shows the highest postitive cases with the numbers trailing off as the year warms up.

In New York State there was a spike in positve cases in Jaunuary 2021. The seven-day average of positive covid tests was also significantly higher in January 2021 than any other month recorded that year
